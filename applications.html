Applications of Machine Learning

1. Healthcare

ML is used to analyze medical data, assist in diagnosis, and predict patient outcomes. Examples include detecting diseases from medical images and personalizing treatment plans.

2. Self-Driving Cars

ML powers autonomous vehicles by analyzing sensor data to detect objects, make decisions, and navigate safely on roads.

3. Recommendation Systems

Platforms like Netflix, YouTube, and Spotify use ML to recommend movies, videos, and music based on user preferences.

4. Fraud Detection

Banks and credit companies use ML algorithms to detect unusual transactions and prevent fraud.

5. Natural Language Processing (NLP)

Voice assistants like Siri, Alexa, and Google Assistant rely on ML to understand and respond to human language.
