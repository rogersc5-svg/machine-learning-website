How Machine Learning Is Used Today

1. Healthcare

Machine Learning helps doctors and hospitals figure out medical stuff faster. For example, it can spot diseases in medical images or help create treatment plans that fit each patient better.

2. Self-Driving Cars

ML is what makes self-driving cars actually drive themselves. It looks at data from sensors to see obstacles, decide what to do, and stay safe on the road.

3. Recommendation Systems

Ever wondered how Netflix, YouTube, or Spotify seem to know what you like? ML studies what you watch or listen to and recommends new stuff you might enjoy.

4. Fraud Detection

Banks use ML to spot weird or suspicious activity in accounts so they can prevent fraud before it happens.

5. Voice Assistants (NLP)

Your voice assistants, like Siri, Alexa, or Google Assistant, use ML to understand what you’re saying and respond correctly. Pretty cool, right?
